About Us

Welcome to WiseAssist, a compassionate platform dedicated to fostering connections and making a positive impact in the lives of our elderly community. At the heart of our mission is a commitment to providing assistance to both seniors and the selfless volunteers who share in our vision.



Our location

Currently our centers span around 6 locations in Mumbai with the main centre located in Chembur and we aim to expand it to more cities in the near future



Our Mission

At WiseAssist, our mission is simple yet profound. We strive to empower the elderly by facilitating volunteer opportunities in old age centers and homes. Through meaningful connections and acts of kindness, we aim to bring joy, companionship, and support to the golden years of our elders. By connecting passionate volunteers with opportunities to make a difference, we foster a community driven by empathy, respect, and a shared commitment to enriching the lives of those who have paved the way for us.



Making a difference

    2. Explore our platform to discover a plethora of volunteering opportunities at various old age centers and homes. Our admins carefully curate and post meaningful roles, ensuring that volunteers can contribute their time and skills where they matter most.

  3. Donation Section

    4. Your contributions make a world of difference. Our donation section provides a platform for individuals to contribute to the cause. Connect With Us:

Feel free to reach out to our administrators for any inquiries, job postings, or collaborations. Together, let's create a world where the elderly are celebrated, volunteers are valued, and kindness knows no bounds.
